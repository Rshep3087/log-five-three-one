About Strength Log


Strength log was created to make logging your strength workouts simple and fast. Many workout logging applications are too general for strength athletes. Too many choices or settings keeps the focus off what is important. Training!



Strength Log is ideal for athletes who train in similar ways to popular programs such as 5/3/1, The Texas Method, GZCL, etc.. Strength Log assumes you include a warm up, a main strength lift, accessory/assistance lifts and conditioning each and every day.



Have questions about Strength Log? Want to share your ideas on improving Strength Log? Contact Strength Log at contact.strength.log@gmail.com. Strength Log wants to hear from you!
